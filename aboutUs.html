Following where He leads  

Our heart has always been to see the good news of Christ to the people who need to hear it. This has led us down a road that took us places we never expected. We have served at SIM International and SIM USA where we got to interact and support missionaries serving in every part of the world. We ministered with a local church in Cardiff, Wales as we helped it transition to a new pastor. Currently, we are in South East London working with two ministries: replanting a church and reaching refugees.

Church Replant

Located in a small village in Kent, Eynsford Baptist Church knew it needed a change and reached out to the church we are currently partnered with, Stone Baptist Church. After much discussion, Stone Baptist has agreed to take over Eynsford Baptist in hopes of revitalizing the church and to make it a beacon of light for the community it finds itself in.

HowWillTheyHear

There are currently over 7 million refugees in Europe. With the hope of building bridges to bring refugees into the kingdom of God, HowWillTheyHear seeks to bring together individuals, churches, ministries, and organizations to serve those who are longing for a better life. Shelbey helps accomplish this goal through his role as communications coordinator.
